# Elfster AI — Project Overview

## What this is

This project is a **prototype** of an AI-powered gifting platform, inspired by [Elfster](https://www.elfster.com). Users describe who they are shopping for and what they need in plain language. The system suggests **gift ideas** that fit those details and can also produce **SEO-friendly content** (clear titles and descriptions) so ideas are easier to discover and understand online.

## Problems we focus on

1. **Budget-constrained gift discovery** — Find thoughtful options that respect a price range, without endless scrolling or guesswork.
2. **Interest and personality-based matching** — Align suggestions with hobbies, tastes, and “vibe,” instead of generic one-size-fits-all lists.
3. **Occasion, group, and seasonal planning** — Support real situations: birthdays, holidays, office exchanges, families, and seasonal themes.

## Why AI

AI acts as a **helper**: it reads open-ended requests, narrows down useful directions, and drafts readable copy. The aim is a simpler path from “I have no idea” to “here are a few solid options”—no technical background required.

## Scope

This is an early **exploration**. Features and direction may evolve as the prototype grows.
